
# Dot Home — Shopping List for Pilot Units (RTX 3060 standard) — v2

- [ ] **GPU** — **GeForce RTX 3060 12 GB**, compact 2‑slot:  
      • ASUS **Dual RTX 3060 V2** *(~200×123×38 mm)* **or** MSI **Ventus 2X RTX 3060** *(235×124×42 mm)* **or** Gigabyte **Windforce OC** *(<200 mm)*

## Option C — **Lian Li A3 mATX** (compact premium tower, replaces P40)

**Why this option:** similar total cost to P40 once VAT/import is included, but with **better build quality, modular PSU mount (ATX/SFX), dust filters and documented airflow**. Still compact (≈26 L), but far easier to cool quietly than tiny ITX cubes.

**Key case specs:** GPU up to **~415 mm** (varies with PSU placement); CPU cooler **≤ 165–167 mm**; supports **ATX/SFX/SFX‑L** PSUs; **no fans included**.

**Buy these (pairings):**
- [ ] **Case:** **Lian Li A3 mATX** (black/wood edition options) — target **~£70–£90** retail  
- [ ] **Motherboard (AM4, mATX)** — **MSI B550M PRO‑VDH WiFi** **or** **Gigabyte B550M DS3H** — ~**£95–£150** used/refurb  
- [ ] **CPU:** **Ryzen 5 5600** — ~**£80–£110** used (or **Ryzen 5 7600** on AM5 if you want DDR5)  
- [ ] **Cooler:** **Thermalright Peerless/Phantom Spirit 120** (~155 mm) — ~**£25–£35** used (or Wraith to save £)  
- [ ] **GPU:** **RTX 3060 12 GB** (ASUS Dual / MSI Ventus / Gigabyte Windforce) — ~**£180–£200** used  
- [ ] **Memory:** **32 GB (2×16) DDR4‑3200/3600** — ~**£90–£95** used (or **DDR5‑6000 EXPO** if AM5)  
- [ ] **Storage:** **2 TB NVMe** (WD **SN770** / Crucial **P3 Plus**) — ~**£90–£115** used/new‑open  
- [ ] **PSU:** **Corsair CX650M** (ATX 140 mm) — ~**£40–£60** used (or SFX + bracket if you prefer tidy cabling)  
- [ ] **Fans:** budget **3–6× 120 mm PWM** (quiet) — e.g., **3× top exhaust, 2–3× bottom intake, 1× rear exhaust** — **£25–£60** total

**Airflow plan (air only):**
- **Top:** 3×120 mm **exhaust**  
- **Bottom:** 2–3×120 mm **intake**  
- **Rear:** 1×120 mm **exhaust**  
- **Side (optional):** 1–2×120 mm **intake** aimed at GPU if needed

**Estimated total (typical private‑seller mix):**  
- With nice tower cooler & 5 fans: **~£700–£780**  
- With Wraith & minimal fans: **~£660–£710**

---

## Quick BIOS checklist

- [ ] Enable **EXPO** (DDR5‑6000) on AM5 / **XMP** or manual 3200/3600 on AM4  
- [ ] Set PEG slot to **Gen4** (Fractal Mood riser)  
- [ ] Enable **Resizable BAR**  
- [ ] Fan curves: Quiet profile; top/front fans ~35–40% until 60 °C

---

## Quiet‑tuning (optional)

- RTX 3060 power cap to **~170 W**  
  - Linux: `sudo nvidia-smi -pl 170`  
  - Windows: Afterburner → Power Limit **85–90%**, slight undervolt

---

### Notes
- Swap any brand for an equivalent **that matches the dimensions and power limits** above.  
- For the Mood case, stick to **true 2‑slot** or **~2.2‑slot** RTX 3060 cards to maintain airflow.
